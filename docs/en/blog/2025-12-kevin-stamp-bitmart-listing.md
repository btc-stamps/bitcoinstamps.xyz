# KEVIN and STAMP List on BitMart

On December 23, 2025, BitMart opened spot trading for two <EntityMention entity="bitcoin-stamps">Bitcoin Stamps</EntityMention> <EntityMention entity="src-20">SRC-20</EntityMention> tokens: <EntityMention entity="kevin" variant="cultural">KEVIN</EntityMention> and STAMP. Both trade against USDT (KEVIN/USDT and STAMP/USDT) in BitMart's Meme and Innovation zone.

Trading opened at 09:00 UTC on December 23, with withdrawals following at 09:00 UTC the next day. BitMart lists the pairs as a primary listing (announcements for [KEVIN](https://bitmart.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/44674823250971--Primary-Listing-BitMart-Will-List-Bitcoin-Stamps-SRC-20-KEVIN-2025-12-23) and [STAMP](https://bitmart.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/44675420348827--Primary-Listing-BitMart-Will-List-Bitcoin-Stamps-SRC-20-STAMP-2025-12-23)), and notes the listing is restricted for residents of Lithuania.

## Why this matters

<EntityMention entity="src-20">SRC-20</EntityMention> tokens store their data in Bitcoin's UTXO set, which is what makes them permanent and unprunable. Trading has always been possible on the native venues that read the chain directly. What's new here is a centralized exchange opening order books for two of these tokens, and doing it for both the protocol's founding token and the STAMP token at once.

We won't dress this up with volume numbers. Liquidity on young pairs is thin and comes and goes, and that's the honest picture. The milestone is simpler: tokens that live permanently on Bitcoin can now be bought and sold on a mainstream exchange, next to the native markets where they already trade.

## KEVIN, briefly

<EntityMention entity="kevin" variant="cultural">KEVIN</EntityMention> is the first SRC-20 token, so it carries the story well. It began as art, Stamp #4258 at block 783,718 on April 3, 2023, a homage to Rare Pepe culture that started replicating across the system on its own. <EntityMention entity="arwyn">Arwyn</EntityMention> then gave that energy a permanent form: on May 3, 2023, at block 788,041, <EntityMention entity="kevin" variant="cultural">KEVIN</EntityMention> was deployed as the first SRC-20 token (Stamp #18,516). It now has more than 2,300 holders.
