# KEVIN and STAMP List on BitMart

<EntityMention entity="kevin" variant="cultural">KEVIN</EntityMention> made it to a centralized exchange. On December 23, 2025, BitMart opened spot trading for two <EntityMention entity="bitcoin-stamps">Bitcoin Stamps</EntityMention> <EntityMention entity="src-20">SRC-20</EntityMention> tokens, <EntityMention entity="kevin" variant="cultural">KEVIN</EntityMention> and STAMP, both against USDT (KEVIN/USDT and STAMP/USDT) in its Meme and Innovation zone.

Trading opened at 09:00 UTC on December 23, with withdrawals following at 09:00 UTC the next day. BitMart carries the pairs as a primary listing (announcements for [KEVIN](https://bitmart.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/44674823250971--Primary-Listing-BitMart-Will-List-Bitcoin-Stamps-SRC-20-KEVIN-2025-12-23) and [STAMP](https://bitmart.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/44675420348827--Primary-Listing-BitMart-Will-List-Bitcoin-Stamps-SRC-20-STAMP-2025-12-23)), and notes the listing is restricted for residents of Lithuania.

## Why this matters

<EntityMention entity="src-20">SRC-20</EntityMention> tokens store their data in Bitcoin's UTXO set, which is what makes them permanent and unprunable. They've always traded on the native venues that read the chain directly. Now a centralized exchange has opened order books for two of them at once: the protocol's founding token and the STAMP token.

Tokens that live permanently on Bitcoin can now be bought and sold on a mainstream exchange, next to the native markets where they already trade.

## KEVIN, briefly

<EntityMention entity="kevin" variant="cultural">KEVIN</EntityMention> is the first SRC-20 token, so it carries the story well. It began as art: Stamp #4258 at block 783,718 on April 3, 2023, a homage to Rare Pepe culture that started replicating across the system on its own. <EntityMention entity="arwyn">Arwyn</EntityMention> then gave that energy a permanent form, deploying <EntityMention entity="kevin" variant="cultural">KEVIN</EntityMention> as the first SRC-20 token (Stamp #18,516) on May 3, 2023, at block 788,041. Today it counts more than 2,300 holders.
