# Code of Conduct

The Bitcoin Stamps community builds together in the spirit of **"In Lak'ech Ala K'in"**: I am another you.

## Our standards

- Treat every contributor with respect. Assume good faith.
- Value authentic contribution over speculation, hype, or self-promotion.
- Give specific, constructive feedback. Critique the work, not the person.
- Preserve the cultural heritage of the project (KEVIN, the Original Trinity, Rare Pepe lineage) with accuracy and care.
- Keep protocol claims honest and sourced. Do not misrepresent technical facts.

## Not acceptable

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination of any kind.
- Spam, scams, or misleading financial claims.
- Deliberately introducing incorrect protocol data or breaking cultural/historical accuracy.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, label, or close contributions that violate these standards, and may remove contributors who repeatedly or seriously breach them. Report concerns via the community channels listed in the README or CONTRIBUTING.

*We are all Kevin. We build together.*
